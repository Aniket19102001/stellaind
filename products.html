Our Products

SCREW

Screws are a type of threaded fastener that includes bolts and studs, but like other fasteners come in a range of different types. Some are for wood and adhere to the wooden surfaces, while others are more suited for machines. Threading for screws can be tapered, straight or parallel.

ALUMINIUM LUGS

ECL aluminium lugs are manufactured from solid rod using high conductivity aluminium. A these lugs are forged from solid material, they are suitable for outdoor use as the palm ends are sealed. Crimping is carried out with standard hex dies and normal aluminium cable preparation.

TERMINAL LINKS

These Brass Terminal Blocks are strong and able to withstand corrosion because they are made from free-cutting brass. For the transmission of electricity, switches and switchgear commonly use brass terminal blocks

MOTORISED SHAFTS & PINS

A motor shaft is a rod-like structure, typically cylindrical, extending from the motor's interior. The shaft's primary function is to transfer the motor's mechanical power – generated within the motor – to other devices or systems.

BRASS INSERTS

Brass Inserts for wood & Special Anchors for Marble are produced in various designs and specifications. Our Brass inserts for wood have an external thread which is suitable to wood where no thread is available.These inserts have an external thread with knife edges which grip deeply into the wood and keep the insert securely in place.
